It was the first night I ever spent away from home and my first tornado. I was four.

All I knew about tornadoes I had learned from The Wizard of Oz. I thought the house was going to fly away and my cousin could not convince me otherwise.

I grew up in Kansas, so it was far from my last tornado. They never scared me like that first one. That one gave me nightmares for the rest of my childhood.
